      2005-0000 Poem ‘Dear Holy Mother’ by the French Collective Presented at 2005-0828 Śhrī Kṛiṣhṇa Pūjā, NJ, U.S.A. from the 2005-1125 and 1202 The Australian Sahaja Yoga Newsletter, Page 1

      1 minute read

      Tags: Poetry French Collective 2005 TBD 2005-1125-1202 The Sahaja Yoga Australian Newsletter Dear Holy Mother


      When Your children listen delicately
      to the perfect pink peace of morning,
      we hear in the timeless solace
      that all is in celebration of Your Name.

      When Your children pass meekly upon
      the footsteps of history’s rugged roads,
      we witness the years of sweet discovery
      seeking ever to arrive at this Divine Hour.

      When Your children follow gently
      the whispers of angels dancing in Your Hands,
      the light of each reflection becomes a blessing,
      and we discover all that there is to Believe.

      When Your children melt into clouds
      of music in the company of heaven,
      we understand that in this world we are one
      and that now we play in no ordinary moment.

      When Your children touch the golden breeze
      and fly above the meagre into Your Blaze,
      we inhale the Vision eternal and receive
      all the faith we shall ever need.

      When Your children bow before You
      and offer flowers in the making of our hearts
      we are fragrant and heavy with the desire
      to create anew Your Land of Grace.

      When Your children stand and share in Truth
      this magic that words can never speak,
      our conviction bears the gravity of every
      monument declaring Your Infinity.

      When Your children feel dearly
      all the beauty upon these ripples of creation,
      we each become the depth that we sense
      and embrace our place in Your Story.

      When we realise in hushed gratitude
      the adventure of these rich and sacred days,
      we know that we are worthy of Your choosing
      and find the courage to be Your children.
