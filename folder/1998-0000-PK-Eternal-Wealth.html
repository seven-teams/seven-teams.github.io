      1998-0000 Poem ‘Eternal Wealth’ by Pavan Keetley from 1998-0905 Australian Sahaja Newsletter, Page 4

      3 minute read

      Tags: Poetry Pavan Keetley 1998 1998-0905 Australian Sahaja Newsletter Story of Poem Eternal Wealth Eternal Wealth


      The question of material wealth
      a spectred myth that haunts by stealth
      the minds, desires of mortal man
      consumes the heart and thwarts the Plan

      For what is Man, his destination?
      and what The Plan, what explanation?
      and what our Seeking, what our Need?
      and why this vast material greed?

      These gross desires for more and more
      are but a shadow I am sure
      of that deepest Primordial Fire
      that raised us all with pure desire

      For we are not just low and base
      our Source is from a higher place
      and as we grow, evolve together
      our Worth is not our fiscal measure

      Our needs are truly very small
      yet wants go on, o'ershadow all
      and yet our greatest need is not
      Fulfilled by all these things we've got

      Aversions are the things that scar
      attachments are the things that bar
      us from That Moment ~ in the Now
      Freedom is free of these somehow

      No no, say no, to all impure
      let go, let God, and so endure
      silence desires when they shout,
      your Self's within ~ even without

      Yet burdens might a blessing be-
      are undulations rocking thee?
      Or are you Rock, unmoved, unbound
      as these illusions crash around?

      A measure of ourselves you see
      is just how peaceful we can be
      when pushed about by our inflations
      by myths and Maya- Life's undulations

      We've come to Earth to Learn to Love
      Her Grace is raining from above
      as empty Cups we can be filled
      yet in God's time, as He so willed

      And when at last we let it go
      commit ourselves to God and so
      relinquish the myth of self-control
      then God takes over life and Soul

      And when our Spirit becomes the Doer
      when we do nothing, we are pure
      immortal hollow reed we'll be
      then as He plays us we will see

      That all that's gone and been before
      and all the myths that we endure
      were there because we had false needs
      tormented by insatiable greeds

      And yet alone untouched and true
      that Pure Desire that slept in you
      witnessed desires that made you bleed
      lay dormant as your Silent Seed

      Until that great and promised day
      when you awoke to truly say
      "My God I am Thy grateful son
      surrendered now ~ Thy Will Be Done"

      This was no shortage you have had
      these undulations you'll be glad
      have showered you now with a grin
      you've found without, our Wealth's within

      The myth of having more or less
      was just a drama now confess
      for in your Self, you truly see
      Source always was protecting thee.

      Our whole life's purpose is Divine
      sometimes we Grapes are pressed for Wine
      that He may quaff our Bliss to see
      how Shakti the Shiva, shines in me ~

      To Realise God, our Spirit pure
      full freedom, choice, we must endure
      until at last, when truly Free,
      God's own Reflection, we must Be

      Then money matters not to thee
      thy Heart open, always will be,
      and She fulfills, Eternal Youth ~
      Eternal Wealth ~ Eternal Truth ~.


      SourcesS1. 1998-0905 Australian Sahaja Newsletter, Page 4, link Archive Post 16, Part 2.
