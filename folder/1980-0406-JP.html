      Poem by John Peirson Offered to Śhrī Mātājī during 1980-0406 Easter Pūjā, Dollis Hill, UK

      less than 1 minute read

      Tags: Poetry John Peirson 1980 UK 1983-0100 and 0200 Nirmala Yoga


      Mother,
      Your Name is our Truth
      Your Love is our Support

      Mother,
      Your Words fill us with Joy
      May our Joy fill every Universe

      Mother
      W hat May we do but Serve You?
      What should we do but praise You?
      What can we do without You?

      Mother,
      Most Glorious You are
      And forever
      Mataji Nirmala Devi.
