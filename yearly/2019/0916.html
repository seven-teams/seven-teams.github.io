      Togliatti Is A Special Place And Russia Is The Highest

      "Togliatti is a special place. Uh so many members we have. Uh I think, I do not know if there are more than [in] Moscow or not, but they are such a lot for this place. Is very surprising [laughs]. [Laughs again.] Very uh surprising thing because people are working in a motor company and the living with such people and then you find them so spiritually strong.

      Yuri Vasiliev [translates the mayor]: Uh why it is happening Śhrī Mātājī? What is Your opinion?

      Śhrī Mātājī: Is something, I think the Volga River's mh uh meeting the sea or whatever it is, it's a very uh holy auspicious place.
      ......
      Yuri Vasiliev [translates the mayor]: You may correct me later is [if] I am wrong. Uh because it is a very new town, very new city. In fact it is several years ago it was eleven thousands [11,000] of citizens only. But now it's seven hundred [thousand] people, Śhrī Mātājī. So, these people love this place, this town, or city now, is very much, Śhrī Mātājī.

      Śhrī Mātājī: Yeah.

      Yuri Vasiliev [translates the mayor]: It may be, maybe, from ... from the love of this.

      Śhrī Mātājī: In the whole of Russia ...

      Yuri Vasiliev: Yes.

      Śhrī Mātājī: In the whole of Russia, uh uh I must say that -- of course Indians know what it is and all that, so we forget India -- but the highest are in Russia. In the whole world now there are sixty-five nations I've [I am] working, Russia is the highest.
