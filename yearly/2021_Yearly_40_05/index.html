      Not To Be Collective Itself Is A Negativity

      "But today in the city of Vancouver, we have gathered here to pray for ... the country of Canada, and also for the Canadian people.
      Also, we have to know that there're lots of negative forces working, and there has to be some Pūjā to be done, to the Mahākālī Force. And so I thought today: 'Let's have the Pūjā for Mahākālī.'
      ......
      And today's prayer should be, in our hearts, that, 'Let us uh get very powerful Gaṇeśha Śhakti. Let us get that.' And second should be that: 'This Śhakti should destroy all that is negativity.'
      Even not to be collective itself is a negativity. All these collectivity problems, or all these negativity problems, must be destroyed by this Mahākālī Power, which will manifest through us."
      1989-0617 Śhrī Mahākālī Pūjā, Āśhram, Keith Road, Ambleside, West Vancouver, BC, Canada
