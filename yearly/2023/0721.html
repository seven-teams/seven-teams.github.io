      2023 Birthday Centenary of H. H. Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi, Post 23 on the Lotus Feet Day (the day when in 2006-0721 we celebrated 1000 Months of Love)

      less than 1 minute read

      Tags: Birthday Centenary






      Paradise Lies At The Feet Of The Mother

      "Muhammad Sāhab said, "Heaven lies at the Feet of the Mother." There is nothing beyond them, meditate on them. Day and night they work very hard to absorb your ego. Look at My Feet, how swollen they are, but I cannot stop them from absorbing your catches because they are very compassionate."
      1900-0000 Quote on Subtler Ego from the book Insights, Inspirations and Eternal Moments, Chapter 39, Pages 122 by Yogi Mahajan

      "Even Muhammad Sāhab has said: that, "Paradise lies at the Feet of the Mother!"
      In the Gospel of Thomas, he writes, authentically: Christ said, that, "This is the Mother who has bore Me in Her womb. But I have another One with a great M capital." "
      1992-0728 Public Program, Budapest, Hungary

      Jay Śhrī Mātājī!
