      Marathi Letters from the Divine Message, Version 8.1

      less than 1 minute read

      Published: May 14, 2023

      Dear Sahaja Yogis and Sahaja Yoginīs,

      We have been notified by the Photo Subteam that May 10 and May 11, 2023, were the first days so far when the Eternal Moments got more than 100K views in a day (1 Photo/photo viewed counts as 1 view), and this was mainly due to dozens of thousands of views of the Handwritings galleries. As such, we felt extremely inspired and highly motivated to share with you all a slim pdf file of just 28 mb with all the 31 Marathi Letters that are currently in our database and that, of course, will be part of the Divine Message, Version 8.1 which will be shared next Sunday (most of them were also part of the earlier versions of Divine Message). The pdf file is available at the link Marathi Letters, from Version 8.1.

      We will keep sharing such files whenever new letters or new translations are added to it.

      Please kindly share the link with all our brothers and sisters.

      Jay Śhrī Mātājī!

      The Divine Message Subteam
