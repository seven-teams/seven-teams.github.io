"Words fail to express the feelings-s-s of such jubilation and such sincere feeling of happiness.<br>
......<br>
With care and with nice things to say we can improve the depth of the collectivity, and with sincere desire to be collective. The desire to be collective has to be very sincere.<br>
So, this expanse-ss of your being will start.<br>
The first thing is needed: a sincerity to yourself.<br>
......"<br>
If you are sincere to any purpose, anything, then you forget the time, you forget the labor, you forget everything: you want to achieve it. Even in small things.<br>
Now, this sincerity comes from where? There are two things-ss which work out sincerity.<br>
Firstly, you must see, for yourself, what is Sahaja Yoga, what it has given you. It has given you Realization, it has given you that wider vision, it has given you Collective Consciousness, it has given you thoughtless awareness and doubtless awareness. It has made a new personality out of you, like an egg becoming a bird.<br>
......<br>
But the second part is different: where you see something, you know something, and you start opening your heart about it. In the second part you have to open your heart. So the sincerity comes from an open heart. If your heart is not open, you cannot be sincere.<br>
Now, what does it mean that your heart is not open? Let us see. You are born-again, you have got your Realization, also it is your birthday in a way. But, in the growth of your knowledge and understanding, you have not kept pace with your heart.<br>
But what is the reason for that? What is it that keeps your heart like that?<br>
You can discover very easily that your heart rules the body, rules your brain, everything. Because if your heart stops, everything stops. But if your brain stops, heart won't stop. So heart is the most important thing, and it governs the whole being within you.<br>
Now this-s special instrument, which is very delicate, which circulates also, is like a stone, how will it pulsate? So when we say, 'Enlarge your heart,' it means that the stoniness of your heart has to be melted away.<br>
