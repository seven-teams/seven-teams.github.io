<font size="+2"><b>Say Sweet Things Where The Children Understand It</b><br></font>
</p>

<p>
"Righteousness must have love and sweetness. Otherwise [is] like a sugar cane which is with ... without any juice. It should not be artificial, of course, but to say sweet things where the children understand it, sweetness, very well.<br>
......<br>
[Before leaving, related to the shawl on which Her Lotus Feet rest:] Who is spoiling their shawl? [Laughter.] Matches with My sāṛī's [color]!" [Laughter.]<br>
