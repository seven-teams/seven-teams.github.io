<font size="+2"><b>You Should All Get My Powers</b><br></font>
</p>

<p>
"Sadāśhiva is the God Almighty, the One who sees the play of Ādi Śhakti. But He is the Father who is watching each one of His Creation, or Her Creation.<br>
His support to Ādi Śhakti is entirely, fully, strengthening. There's no doubt in His mind about the capability of the Ādi Śhakti. But when He finds that in the play of Ādi Śhakti, the people or the world as such, try to disturb Her or spoil Her work, then He gets into His wrathful mood, and He destroys all such people, and maybe, He destroys the whole world.<br>
......<br>
If you cannot surrender, you can never know God.
......<br>
What I want is: you all should all get My Powers. You may not become what I am, I agree, but please try to get all the powers that I have, which is not a difficult thing.<br>
This is what is God's Realization, that's what is to know Śhiva and Sadāśhiva."<br>
<font color="blue"><b>1996-0303 Śhivarātri Pūjā, Motivate Your Attention or God's Realization, Bundilla Scout Camp, 6 Baden Powell Pl, Winston Hills (35 kms E of Sydney), New South Wales, Australia</b></font><br>
