"And whatever you find out, like, `This is such a comfortable dress for us,' you'd better do it! Because what I have seen it ... in eeh ... many, you see, it's a thing that, whenever I tell you something, don't take it to the extremes and don't take a little bit of it. But, when I tell you this thing, you must understand, that: whatever is good for you, whatever is your temperament, whatever is [your] aptitude, you have to take something that will nourish that, that will improve you, and give you a balance.<br>
So, to balance yourself, if you think of Mahāvīra, you must think also of Śhi... Śhrī Hanumān. Both these things are important."<br>

"So we have to thank, really, very much Mahāvīra, who has given us this power to remove our left-sided problems, who has given us-ss this light in our hand, which He Himself vibrates and He Himself tries to throw some light on the obscure things about Me also. Because of Him only, we can get cured of ss-so many physical and mental troubles.<br>
You know that the left side can be easily cured if you use three-candle treatment.<br>
That shows Mahāvīra is only fond of light. He doesn't like darkness.<br>
And His idea is, I think that way, is to now fully go all the way to clear the left side as far as possible, to help Sahaja Yoga to be established in countries where I find still lot of this negativity is there -- lot of it.<br>
So He's working it out, everywhere.<br>
And the psychosomatic diseases and all these diseases also we can work it out with candle, light and all that: with three candle[s] we can do this treatment.<br>
So, you have to be very thankful to Him that He has achieved this kind of entry into the left side and has found out for us what sort of problems-ss exist on left side.<br>
Is such a nice thing that we have got, today, the chance to celebrate His birthday here in a place which is really very much loved by Him."<br>
<font color="blue"><b>1991-0328 Śhrī Mahāvīra Pūjā, Left Side and Soul, Gidgegannup (40 kms NE of Perth), Darling Ranges, WA, Australia</b></font><br>
