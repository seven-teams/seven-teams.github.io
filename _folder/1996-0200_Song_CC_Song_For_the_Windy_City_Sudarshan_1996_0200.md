<p>
Grey clouds were covering the Windy City of Chicago,<br>
Keeping the Light of the Spirit away from the seekers' hearts.<br>
The Lake, the Earth, Air and Fire, together with the Angels,<br>
Joined their forces to dispel the clouds of ignorance<br>
And purify the battlefield. The fight went on without respite,<br>
Till the blessed day You came, Oh Mother,<br>
In the form of Jagadamba, to rescue all Your children.<br>
<br>
From the day when Your Foot touched its ground,<br>
The city has rejoiced in the blessings of Your Protection.<br>
For two days and nights You gave a fierce battle<br>
To all our fears and our worries and our foes.<br>
Incessantly You fought with alt Your blazing weapons.<br>
And Your Sudarshan Chakra was doom to all the evil forces.<br>
They were consumed by the fire of Your Tapasya<br>
And Your Light of Compassion and of Pure Love pervaded everything,<br>
Shining like a thousand suns on the shores of the Windy City.<br>
<br>
White-purple clouds are now enjoying themselves in the sky<br>
While the Wind of the Holy Spirit gently blows over the city.<br>
The Lake, the Earth, Air and Fire, together with the Angels,<br>
Have joined their hands in a dance celebrating Your Victory.<br>
The flowers have absorbed the fragrance of Your Presence<br>
And are joyfully giving it back to everyone.<br>
Oh Mother, the Nature is so beautiful now, here in the Windy City,<br>
The Angels, the Elements and all Your children<br>
Are filed with the joy of exalted expectation<br>
As we humbly await and pray that You grace is again with Your Darshan<br>
Oh Mother, the Windy City is longing for the touch.<br>
Of Your compassionate, joy-giving Lotus Feet.
</p>
