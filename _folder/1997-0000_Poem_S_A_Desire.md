<p>
I have never seen you,<br>
Neither I know you,<br>
How can I know in what form you are?<br>
You seem to be out of my reach,<br>
Beyond my imagination,<br>
I know you only,<br>
By a beautiful photograph,<br>
By a few lectures,<br>
Some well known stories,<br>
On tongue tip of seekers,<br>
I wish to come to you,<br>
To listen to a few words only meant for me,<br>
A ray of hope - a lovely waiting is there in my mind,<br>
A candle of faith in my heart,<br>
I always keep burning,<br>
Your awe surrounds me,<br>
To listen to miraculous stories,<br>
I shiver with excitement,<br>
To enjoy your unending love,<br>
I crave for day and night,<br>
For a few drops of love, generosity,<br>
A wave of vibrations I surrender to your lotus feet,<br>
Make me a part and parcel of the whole,<br>
Bestow upon me a touch of your hand,<br>
So that I ascend the stairs of the Divine.
</p>
