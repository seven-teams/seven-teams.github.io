<p style="text-align:center;">
Why is it<br>
that truth is often told to deceive?<br>
Truth is often incomplete<br>
and rarely knowledge that is pure?<br>
<br>
Honesty springs from wisdom and from innocence<br>
<br>
Honesty spreads knowledge<br>
that can care<br>
<br>
Honesty brings trust to human life<br>
<br>
Honesty is taught by the Mother<br>
Honesty is demanded by the Father<br>
Honesty keeps me on the path to Truth
</p>
