<p style="text-align:center;">
Fast pace of life - where does it lead?<br>
Fast pace of thinking - is this me?<br>
Fast pace of love - can this be?<br>
<br>
My heart is longing for thee<br>
Quietly and steadily<br>
For the beauty and wisdom of thee<br>
<br>
There is no hurry – not at all<br>
<br>
Love needs time<br>
to penetrate the heart<br>
Love needs time<br>
to create the joy of romantic art<br>
<br>
Like a Mother slowly sees her child<br>
growing, developing<br>
needing to be cared for<br>
Gently and mild
</p>
