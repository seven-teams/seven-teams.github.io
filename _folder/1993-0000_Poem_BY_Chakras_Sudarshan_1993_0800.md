<br>

<p>
i listen to the wheels<br>
wheels that never stop turning<br>
<br>
wheels that know<br>
my sins<br>
and my merits<br>
<br>
wheels that watch me<br>
beneath my eyes<br>
<br>
that if i die<br>
the wheels, like echos<br>
will never touch<br>
the earth
</p>
