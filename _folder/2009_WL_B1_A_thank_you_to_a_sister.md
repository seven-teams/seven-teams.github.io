<p style="text-align:center;">
You bow to me<br>
I bow to you<br>
<br>
With rays of love your bandhan is engulfing me<br>
Mother Kundalini streams with joy<br>
<br>
What cleansing flow<br>
What cooling wind<br>
<br>
The lotus opens<br>
Fragrance pure and innocent<br>
flows down on me – reflects on you<br>
<br>
The kumkum silences my thoughts<br>
While the rice poured with such devotion<br>
touches my Mother’s feet<br>
As she dances full of happiness<br>
Dances in the center of the lotus<br>
Dances – blessing me & you<br>
<br>
You give a string which binds me now<br>
in love to you – but wait!<br>
<br>
It binds me not – it frees my heart<br>
Pouring out its love without a need<br>
Without a quest, without a want<br>
<br>
There’s something I want to say – but the<br>
sweetness of the candy keeps me quiet<br>
lets me enjoy the moment<br>
<br>
I bow to you<br>
You bow to me
</p>
