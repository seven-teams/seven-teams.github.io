<p>
I am Peace,<br>
Nothing but peace am I<br>
<br>
I am in peace and harmony<br>
With myself and with the world.<br>
<br>
All worries and tensions are gone<br>
And only peace is all around me now.<br>
<br>
I am still and everything is still<br>
Not even a leaf is moving,<br>
<br>
The Universe is at rest<br>
<br>
Lying down peacefully<br>
In Your hand.
</p>
