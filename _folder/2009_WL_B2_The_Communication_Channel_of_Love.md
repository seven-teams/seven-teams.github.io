<p style="text-align:center;">
The communication channel of love<br>
is a fascinating piece of art<br>
<br>
It has threads of respect,<br>
threads of care and threads of playful banter<br>
Vibrating in wondrous harmony<br>
<br>
If used with honesty and with patience<br>
friend and enemy can respond alike<br>
<br>
Lover or stranger, brother or rival,<br>
ally or competitor<br>
All can have an open ear for it<br>
<br>
Once such a channel is open
it doesn’t easily shut close again<br>
Harmony holds all sides in sync<br>
Leading to a mode of understanding<br>
<br>
This communication channel of love<br>
is such an astounding thing<br>
Creating miracles in configurations<br>
in which no one thought it possible
</p>
