<p>
HAPPY BIRTHDAY O GREAT GODDESS<br> 
(FROM ALL THE YOGIS OF THIS AGE)<br> 
<br>
City to city,<br>
Village to village,<br>
We praise Your Holy Name<br>
For all children one and the same.<br>
Some shall dance,<br>
Some shall sing,<br>
Some blow the conch<br>
And bells will ring!<br>
<br>
Some carry sparks<br>
In their bright eyes<br>
Of Holy Spirit --<br>
All recognize.<br>
<br>
Some gladly bend.<br>
Revering Mother Earth<br>
Loving hands will mend<br>
And announce Her worth.<br>
<br>
Some carry water<br>
Full of vibrations<br>
Their works of art<br>
Pure adorations.<br>
<br>
Some will churn<br>
The ocean deep<br>
For nectar of Moksha<br>
And sorrow will sleep.<br>
<br>
Without fear of defeat<br>
Seeing only Your lotus feet,<br>
Some will take their candlelight<br>
Into the darkest corners of the night.<br>
<br>
City to city,<br>
Village to village,<br>
We proclaim Your Holy Name<br>
For people of all races one and the some.<br>
Some shall dance.<br>
Some shall sing,<br>
Some blow the conch<br>
And the bells will ring.<br>
<br>
"l've got peace like a river, in my soul<br>
l've got joy like a mountain, in my soul<br>
"l've got love like the ocean... In my soul"<br>
"Holy Mother, by Your Love["]<br>
"Each broken child will be mode whole!"
</p>
