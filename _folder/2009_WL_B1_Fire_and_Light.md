<p style="text-align:center;">
Fire and Light<br>
Water and Air<br>
Tenderly are cleansing me<br>
<br>
The clear and open sky<br>
With view of stars<br>
Gently does comfort me<br>
<br>
The Mother in the distance<br>
Lovingly pulls me to her feet<br>
Happy, full of joy, she embraces me
</p>
