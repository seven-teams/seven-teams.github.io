<p style="text-align:center;">
When I close my eyes and quiet<br>
succumbs my mind<br>
I think of air, I think of breath,<br>
I think of food and shelter, it is all there.<br>
I think no more. I am lifted within,<br>
my Spirit moves me, I sway from side to side.<br>
Love, peace and harmony take control,<br>
I am content and glad.<br>
<br>
I am blessed, I know it.<br>
I smile because I have traveled<br>
to a perfect place.<br>
It is called temple of perfection or<br>
temple of truth, it can also be referred to as<br>
Thy sacred body of love and peace...GOD.<br>
I have explored me and now I know myself.<br>
<br>
It is a wonderful absolute exhilarating feeling<br>
to know that I know what I know, absolutely.<br>
I am indeed happy with me<br>
and now I am happy with you.<br>
I have joined in on the celebration of living,<br>
and now it is time for me to go<br>
and spread peace and love forever.
</p>
