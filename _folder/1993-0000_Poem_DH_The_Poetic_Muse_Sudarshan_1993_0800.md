<p>
The Muse in all Her glory stands<br>
Before us, blessings flowing from Her hands<br>
No more, will only whisper in a poet's ear<br>
Now, speaks Her message, sweet and clear,<br>
To thousands, so that all who will, may hear,<br>
"Arise, the golden age before us lies.<br>
See your own glory, and believe your eyes,<br>
Know God in you, together one,<br>
Accomplishing the work of love, long since begun.<br>
Forgive the past, feel the true breath of truth<br>
That is the faithful guardian of your youth,<br>
And blossom to the beauty you were want to be,<br>
As fragrant garden, Earth fulfill destiny."<br>
Now feel the cool vibrations flow<br>
To those who would their spirit know.<br>
Behold, the Mother of this life,<br>
Fountain of peace, the end of worldly strife.<br>
May your own Holy Muse forever be,<br>
Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi.
</p>
