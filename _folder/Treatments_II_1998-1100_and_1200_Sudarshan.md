<ins>Seven Chilies and One Lemon</ins><br>
<br>
For this cleansing technique, you will need seven chilies, one lemon or lime, a plate large enough to hold the chilies and lemon, a small paring knife.<br>
Raise Kundalini and put yourself in bandhan. Vibrate the chilies and lemon by placing them in front of Shri Mataji's photograph or by reciting the three great mantras over them.<br>
As you sit in front of Shri Mataj's photograph, cut the lemon in half. Put one half on the Sahasrara. Hold the other half in your left hand. Hold all seven chilies in your right hand. Sit in thoughtless awareness for 15 minutes, letting the lemon and chilies absorb negativity.<br>
When vibrations feel cool, put the two lemon halves and seven chilies on the plate. Without slicing completely through the rind, make two deep cuts criss-crossing each lemon half. Each lemon half will now be divided into four quarters.<br>
Make four equally spaced cuts across the width of each chili, taking care not to cut completely through the chili. Place the lemon and chilies in a plastic bag and close it.<br>
Wash your hands, and return to meditate in front of the photograph. Thank Shri Mataji in Her Shri Ganesha and Shri Mahalaxmi forms for clearing your chakras. Also thank Shri Mataji in Her Shri Annapurna form for providing the lemon and chilies.<br>
<br>
<ins>Aarti on Wednesday</ins><br>
<br>
Every Wednesday at the Health Center, aarti was performed to Shri Mataj's photo. The photograph was first cleaned with a red silk or cotton cloth moistened with rosewater, moving the cloth in a clockwise motion. Then Shri Mataii's bindi as well as Her Hands and Feet were decorated with kum kum.<br>
The Aarti Saba Ko Dua Dena was sung while giving bandhans to the photo.<br>

<ins>Attention Exercise</ins><br>
<br>
To assist the Kundalini as She works to strengthen our attention, the following exercise was given.<br>
While sitting in meditation, draw the attention up the central channel starting at Mooladhara passing through each chakra to Sahasrara.<br>
Draw the attention down the left side of the head to the top of the left ear, across the brow to the bridge of the nose, down the right
nostril and al the way down the right channel to the right Swadisthana.<br>
Draw the attention back up the central channel and down the right side of the head to the top of the right ear, across the brow to the bridge of the nose, down the left nostril and all the way down the left channel to the Mooladhara Chakra.<br>
Again, take the attention up the central channel ot Saharara. Repeat the exercise, this time going down the right and left arms in turn. Let the attention rest on the palm of the hand each time. Draw the attention back to Sahasrara.<br>
Repeat, this time drawing the attention down the legs, one at a time to the soles of the feet.<br>
Bring the attention up the central channel to Saharara and sit in silent meditation. Enjoy the silence - the precious gift of the Kundalini.<br>
