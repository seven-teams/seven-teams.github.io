<p>

Shri Hanuman, You are the Angel<br>
of Our Mother's heart<br>
You are the messenger of dreams<br>
that come true<br>
Please deliver me<br>
at the threshold of Surrender<br>
and give me strength to enter<br>
without fear.<br>
Please bring me to the depths<br>
of my Spirit for<br>
it is so cold<br>
and lonely<br>
out here<br>
on the surface<br>
all alone...
</p>
