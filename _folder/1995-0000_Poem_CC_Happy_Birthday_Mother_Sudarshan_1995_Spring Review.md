<p>
The Sun is set afire in all his golden glory,<br>
The Mother Earth is peacefully pulsating<br>
Like a heart overwhelmed with love,<br>
The grass and trees vibrate with joy amidst the blooming flowers<br>
Sweetly caressed by the most fragrant breeze<br>
The Universe rejoices in full harmony<br>
The whole of Nature is in utmost bliss<br>
And all the Devas and the Angels dance in ecstasy,<br>
For all of them know that today, Beloved Mother, is Your Birthday.<br>
<br>
Happy Birthday to You
</p>
