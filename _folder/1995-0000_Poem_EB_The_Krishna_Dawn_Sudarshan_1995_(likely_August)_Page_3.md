<p>
Once upon a Sunrise, when the glorious orb<br>
washed the sparrow's flight in pink, adoring fight,<br>
You blurred the edges of 'me', expanding them to reflect Thee.<br>
Your Fluted bird song was met, with joyous spinning duet,<br>
a melody intrinsic to Your gesture Divine,<br>
while the nature of my existence was wholly redefined.<br>
<br>
For who can divide Beauty from Her Source Divine?<br>
Who can sing more softly to the Sun, than gentle Sunshine?<br>
<br>
All the world rests on the sweetness of that moment,<br>
When inside Your Magic Flute, I became a drop of Sunshine.<br>
Although half a world away, from Dwaraka,<br>
your ancient land,<br>
I fear not death or time: You held me in Your Hard.<br>
For time is a mist, death a gate, and joy the Amrut Infinite!<br>
<br>
Inside Your pink down sparrow song, the lucky souls unite.<br>
For when we become Your sacred song,<br>
we melt into oceans of light.<br>
<br>
Krishna Dawn vibrations,<br>
Peacock fan unfold,<br>
glory reflected in simple joy,<br>
She is Infinite Love, behold.
</p>
