<p>
In the valleys of green and brown,<br>
lives the wind; She tells me Her plea<br>
with humankind, as She whistles the song of the Divine.<br>
<br>
She plays a tune on every breath she voices,<br>
as She whispers in my ear the valley's choices.<br>
<br>
She comes to my room, to my thoughts,<br>
to my dreams, and shatters me with love, and beams;<br>
She's mine, intangible to everyone else;<br>
and I, I am that small instrument of someone else.
</p>
