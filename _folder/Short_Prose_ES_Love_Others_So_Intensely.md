<p style="text-align:center;">
We have to love ourselves so that we can love<br>
others. What if, simply by loving people <br>
intensely and unconditionally, we sahaja yogis<br>
could save them, as they then could sincerely<br>
feel and believe in us, and therefore in God,<br>
Shri Mataji. Faith based on enlightened love.<br>
Could this be part of the Divine Plan?<br>
</p>
