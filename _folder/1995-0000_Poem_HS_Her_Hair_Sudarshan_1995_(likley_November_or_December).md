<p style="text-align:center;">
Behold the woman with light in her hair<br>
Diamonds, stars and words kept there<br>
lure sworn she is all of that veils the truth<br>
and lives of those in ancient tales<br>
who horn again, tell the story<br> 
sing the songs and praise her glory<br>
Enmeshed within her flowing locks mysterious<br>
The sky, the wind, the sea, the rocks<br>
The dreams of ages come to bare<br>
in her simple form and long black hair
</p>
