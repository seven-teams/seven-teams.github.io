<p>
When you stop looking for mirrors<br>
in the outside world,<br>
but turn inward,<br>
gazing into the bottomless lake<br>
which is your own heart and spirit,<br>
you might find a diamond,<br>
an essence,<br>
a source,<br>
a quintessential.<br>
<br>
Somewhere invisibly tangible,<br>
underneath the now,<br>
between pure desire and silence,<br>
alight shines in darkness.<br>
<br>
Search with grateful awareness.<br>
Do not fear the endless echos<br>
of your solitary footsteps<br>
in pitch darkness.<br>
<br>
Collect all your hopefulness<br>
to this moment,<br>
this magnetic core,<br>
this speck of infinite.<br>
<br>
Crawl inside and become it.<br>
<br>
Then you will be like the seed<br>
from which springs<br>
the mountains<br>
and the roses.<br>
<br>
Then you will become<br>
one of the lucky singers<br>
in the Garden.
</p>
