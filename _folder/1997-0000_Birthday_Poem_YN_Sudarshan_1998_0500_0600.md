<p>
To Our Holy Mother<br>
We are grateful that you are here<br>
When I look at you, I see a face of love and a heart of joy<br>
Thank you Mother for watching over us<br>
Thank you Mother for guiding us in our lessons<br>
Thank you Mother for teaching me to love plants, animals and people<br>
Thank you Mother, for coming into my heart<br>
You are in my mind and ni everything that I do<br>
Always remembering and loving you, Mother<br>
HAPPY BIRTHDAY<br>
FROM YOUR FLOWER CHILDREN<br>
</p>
