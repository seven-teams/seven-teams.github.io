<p>
Far reaching plains<br>
encircle Her sacred palace<br>
gilded by the mists<br>
of a morning lake.<br>
<br>
Many majestic rooms<br>
weave an intricate world<br>
endless, echoing<br>
walls that sing<br>
a silence<br>
so deep<br>
<br>
hearts open<br>
to Her music<br>
most rare<br>
surrendering<br>
to the Spirit<br>
dwelling<br>
in this Holy Place.
</p>
