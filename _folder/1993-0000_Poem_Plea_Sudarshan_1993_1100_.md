<p>
Let us maintain the momentum generated by the visit of our Mother and assume our position as a true expression of pure collectivity. For this Shri Mataji, we offer this humble plea:<br>
<br>
From the land of limitless bounty,<br>
on whose shores great oceans lie,<br>
a song of love and brotherhood<br>
from the collective heart does rise!<br>
<br>
Our voices blend and meld as one<br>
to say a silent prayer,<br>
that sixteen thousand bhaktas strong<br>
will find true refuge here!<br>
<br>
Our tears have fallen like so much rain<br>
on bleak and barren ground,<br>
but now we see before our eyes<br>
a vast and fruited plain.<br>
<br>
It is you, Oh Mother, who have thrown apart<br>
the doors of heaven wide,<br>
to let your rays of love shine forth,<br>
and beckon us all inside.
</p>
