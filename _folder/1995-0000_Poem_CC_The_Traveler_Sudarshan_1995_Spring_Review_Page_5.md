<p>
I have been across the widest skies<br>
Swimming through the fire of the Sun<br>
Touching the heart of the Moon<br>
With my arms spread to embrace the Universe<br>
I have reached the farthest stars and galaxies<br>
Traveled throughout the worlds and ages<br>
Encompassing Time and Space<br>
And everywhere I have found Your resplending Lotus Feet<br>
Their dust creating whirling universes.<br>
<br>
I have ben inside the nectar of the flower<br>
Floating in the sap of the grass blade,<br>
Playing in frolics amongst the merry molecules<br>
I have penetrated the ultimate heart of the atoms<br>
And touched the aerial garments of the seven Colours<br>
And inside the innermost essence of all things<br>
I have found the Splendour of Your shining Feet<br>
Adorned by the light of the gems<br>
Of which Nature's diadem is made.<br>
<br>
I have penetrated the essence of the mind<br>
I have gone beyond knowledge and ignorance<br>
To reach the nature of Truth<br>
And inside the light of its heart<br>
I have found You, Shri Mother, gracefully smiling in peaceful joy<br>
Protecting and nourishing the whole Creation,<br>
Having the Great Illusion as the cushion under Your feet<br>
And Reality as Your throne.<br>
I have been a traveler of the worlds and ages<br>
And now I have returned home.
</p>
