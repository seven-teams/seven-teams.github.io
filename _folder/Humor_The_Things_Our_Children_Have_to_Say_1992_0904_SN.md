THE THINGS OUR CHILDREN SAY<br>
<br>
The three and a half year old Sahaja Yogi was taken to pre-school by his auntie from the Ashram because his mum had slipped a disc and the chiropractor had put her to bed for two days. She had a physical adjustment and the he used a chiropractors gun to set the last vertebra in place.
"Where's mummy today?" asked the pre-school teacher to Mr 3$1/2$.<br>
"The man shot her!" he replied.<br>
"Pardon?" she said with a slightly shocked look.<br>
"The man shot her!"<br>
"Which man shot her?" she asked.<br>
"THE CRACTA PRACTA!" Mr 3$1/2$ said quite seriously.
