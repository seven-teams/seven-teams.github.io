<p style="text-align:center;">
Sometimes people have to die<br>
Sometimes people just move on<br>
when they know that they are ready<br>
<br>
Nobody is perfect<br>
that is why we are human<br>
We were made to fall<br>
so that we can grow<br>
in Mother’s fertile soil<br>
<br>
It is our ability<br>
to absorb her love that counts<br>
It is our desire to evolve<br>
which makes us look beyond<br>
<br>
It is the knowledge of the soul<br>
which makes us grow higher<br>
Seeking out the truth<br>
<br>
It is the joy<br>
of seeing Mother’s caring smile<br>
which makes us want to give the love received
</p>
