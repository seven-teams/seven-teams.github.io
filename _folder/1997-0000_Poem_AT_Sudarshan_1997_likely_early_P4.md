<p>
It's shining outside,<br>
the United states of America is<br>
showering flowers<br>
And it's beautiful to God.<br>
And the flowers look like Shri Ganesha<br>
And the roses look like Shri Mataji<br>
and the flowers look like poems.<br>
Your Holy Mother is near.<br>
When she was young, long, long time ago<br>
She killed theDemons on Her Tiger.<br>
She loves everybody.
</p>
