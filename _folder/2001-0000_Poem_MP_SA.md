<p>
Twinkle twinkle little star<br>
Jai Shree Mataji from my heart<br>
Up above the world so high<br>
Like vibrations in the sky<br>
Twinkle twinkle little star<br>
Jai Shree Mataji from my heart<br>
Jai Shree Mataji from my Kundalini<br>
Jai Shree Mataji Nirmala Ma.<br>
</p>
