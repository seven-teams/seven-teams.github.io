<p>
One day my Grandfather was driving in his van. He spilt some hot water on his face and burnt him self [himself]. Even the doctor could not do anything about it. Soon after a puja, SHRI MATAJI called him and faced the fan towards his face and cured him. All the burnt marks went away from his face. The doctors were amazed to see that.
</p>
