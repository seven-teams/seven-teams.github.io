<p>
Jai Shri Mataji<br>
How lucky are we<br>
the ones who know<br>
know Islam<br>
know the meaning of Islam<br>
the true meaning of Islam<br> 
I surrender<br>
I surrender my soul<br>
my heart<br>
not only know<br>
but feel<br>
feel surrender to the Devi<br>
the great Devi<br>
Shri Mataji Nirmala Devi<br>
</p>
