<p>
To please, to please our Mother so high,<br>
There never shall be a purpose so fine,<br>
To please, to please our Mother so high,<br>
there never shall be a purpose so fine.<br>
<br>
Mother please come into my heart,<br>
Dwell in the blossom which opens apart.<br>
Sit on your throne 0 Fragrant Goddess,<br>
My Queen so noble, Compassion so tireless.<br>
<br>
In my heart let your feet rest,<br>
So that I may toil to treat them best,<br>
With fragrance and pollens and Extollations,<br>
To those working feet emitting Vibrations.<br>
<br>
Let me be free from delusion,<br>
So that I may see without confusion,<br>
All blessings and joys that you have given me,<br>
I thank you now and eternally.
</p>
