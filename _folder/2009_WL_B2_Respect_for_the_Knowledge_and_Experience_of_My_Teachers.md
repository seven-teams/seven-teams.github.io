<p style="text-align:center;">
The love in my heart can express itself in many forms<br>
Lately, I was wondering about<br>
the love for my teachers<br>
<br>
It is a deep kind of love<br>
Full of gratefulness, full of humility<br>
And full of the sweetest joy, when I can learn<br>
<br>
Its expression is<br>
of respect for knowledge and experience<br>
Its expression is<br>
of trust in the goodness of their teachings<br>
Its expression is<br>
in finding a way to apply their teachings to my life<br>
<br>
My trust in them is not with blindness<br>
Blindness would disrespect their knowledge<br>
<br>
My trust in them is not without knowing of their defects<br>
It’s because their knowledge and experience far<br>
outshine their weaknesses<br>
that my love for them is strong<br>
<br>
It’s because of their imperfections<br>
that I have hope to overcome my own<br>
<br>
My love for the light in them<br>
makes me want to strengthen the light in me<br>
<br>
For that I can become a teacher too
</p>
