<p>
Your Lot Feet are cool and sweet,<br>
Glowing red inside my head.<br>
I cannot say what Y am seeing,<br>
But it's the centre of my Being.<br>
The Glow expands, envelops me,<br>
It's you, not me, Shri Mataji.<br>
</p>
