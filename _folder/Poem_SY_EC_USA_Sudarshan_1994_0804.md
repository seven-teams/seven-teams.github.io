<br>

<p style="text-align:center;">
Sugar and spices.<br>
Breads and Jams<br>
But for Heaven's sake<br>
No shakes, lambs and hams<br>
Porky Pig can go for a dig,<br>
And if you wish you had a dish of fish,<br>
it's time to get over your feverish wish.<br>
We beg you not to have an egg.<br>
Don't be an egghead,<br>
leave them, save them, to keep you warm,<br>
when the heat is gone.
</p>
