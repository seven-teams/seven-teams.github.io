<br>

<p>
There is a thing which I can feel,<br>
It flows through my hands<br>
and my being.<br>
Oh how I rejoice.<br>
<br>
There is something natural,<br>
innocent, inherent, beautiful,<br>
Beyond praise, beyond thought,<br>
formless and all pervading,<br>
I can feel it.<br>
Oh how I rejoice.<br>
<br>
There is something which loves us,<br>
which helps us<br>
It flows in me, around me,<br>
through me<br>
<br>
I am lost in it, immersed in it.<br>
It is the flow of amrit,<br>
it is purusha, it is chaitanya,<br>
<br>
I feel my Self, my true Self<br>
shining forth the brilliance<br>
of a thousand suns.<br>
Oh how I rejoice.<br>
<br>
For this is truly living, for this is<br>
truly life<br>
And I bathe in the ocean of<br>
Mother's love.
</p>
