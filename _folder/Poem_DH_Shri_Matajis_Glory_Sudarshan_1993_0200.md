<p style="text-align:center;">

</p>
Shri Mataji, Your glory is our joy.<br>
To serve you, may we al our wit employ,<br>
And decorate Your throne with all our art,<br>
Thereby to tell the world who rules our heart.<br>
<br>
Pray let us crown You with a crown of gold,<br>
And make our speaches [speeches] of You proud and bold.<br>
For all the world to know the Mother of our soul,<br>
The Queen of Heaven whose kingdom is our goal.<br>
<br>
In angry places far from heaven's gate,<br>
May we the joy of paradise relate,<br>
How in us a garden blooms, a cleansing river flows,<br>
The heavenly flute is heard, the cool breeze blows.<br>
<br>
We breathe the fragrance of Your matchless love,<br>
Are bathed in cool chaitanya from above.<br>
Pray let us decorate Your Holy Feet,<br>
With brimming hearts and myriad roses sweet.<br>
<br>
Such tender blessings fall on us, we yearn to share,<br>
Return Shri Bhoomi Devi's loving care.<br>
And raise Her sick and dying children up<br>
To drink the amrut from the eternal cup.<br>
<br>
Thy kingdom come within this troubled age.<br>
With all Thy powers of love this fight we wage.<br>
Thy will be done, and quickly, by our hands,<br>
To spread Your light throughout the darkened lands.
<br>
