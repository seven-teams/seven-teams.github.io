<p style="text-align:center;">
Love and Joy bubble in my heart when I see you<br>
Love and Joy bubble in my heart when I think of you<br>
Love and Joy bubble in my heart when I touch you<br>
<br>
Love like emanating from the smile of a mother<br>
Joy like celebration in the pride of a father<br>
<br>
Attention commanded by your presence<br>
Action required by your glance<br>
<br>
You are with me in my heart<br>
You are part and parcel of my soul<br>
<br>
I cannot be without you for even a fraction of a moment<br>
<br>
You are with me in my heart<br>
You are part and parcel of my soul
</p>
