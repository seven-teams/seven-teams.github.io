<p>
She who loves us all<br>
Helps us all and<br>
Runs us all<br>
Inside her heart is lots of love<br>
Mataji is the word.<br>
Australian she makes<br>
Them all<br>
And gives them<br>
Just pure<br>
Innocence<br>
</p>
