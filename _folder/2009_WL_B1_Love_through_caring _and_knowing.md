<p style="text-align:center;">
Innocence brings about the wisdom<br>
I have been seeking<br>
<br>
The sun and the moon combine to give balance<br>
Balance to the roller coaster of my life<br>
<br>
The earth rejoices as my heart connects with her<br>
Gently sending out her caring love<br>
As my attention extends roots of grateful embrace<br>
<br>
The motherly flow of knowledge begins to rise<br>
<br>
Understanding envelops my brain<br>
Lifting my heart to unite with the universe<br>
<br>
Oneness has found a home
</p>
