<p>
Radiant red rose,<br>
Blooming on the top of the Universe,<br>
Diamond stud Morning Sun,<br>
Cutting the dark layers away,<br>
Uniquely,<br>
Magnificent magical word,<br>
Opening countless dimensions,<br>
Cool and calm full moon,<br>
Lulling the sick human mind,<br>
Stage of divine Maharas,<br>
Dancing to amazing tunes.<br>
Deep ocean of magnetic love,<br>
Washing the oozing wounds.<br>
Bundle of dazzling auras,<br>
Lighting the whole globe. 
</p>
