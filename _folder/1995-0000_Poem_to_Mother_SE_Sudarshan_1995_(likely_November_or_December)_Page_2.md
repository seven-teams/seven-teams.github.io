<p>
I met a deer<br>
On my way to the core<br>
of the heart<br>
of Mother Nirmala.<br>
<br>
He was clad<br>
With the rug of gold:<br>
The Sari<br>
of Mother Nirmala.<br>
<br>
His eyes sparkling<br>
Made of Diamonds:<br>
The weapons<br>
of Mother Nirmala.<br>
<br>
His speed of light<br>
Against the Wind:<br>
The Vision<br>
Of Mother Nirmala.<br>
<br>
But his touch the best<br>
like soft flowers:<br>
The Breath<br>
of Mother Nirmala.
</p>
