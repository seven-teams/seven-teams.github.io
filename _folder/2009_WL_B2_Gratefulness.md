<p style="text-align:center;">
Love is a feeling and a power<br>
directed to my fellow human beings<br>
Love is a feeling and response<br>
to the greater one<br>
<br>
But more than love<br>
it’s gratefulness<br>
that I wake up with every morning<br>
<br>
Gratefulness<br>
for how far I’ve come<br>
<br>
Gratefulness<br>
for how much I’ve learned<br>
<br>
Gratefulness<br>
to be alive and see<br>
evolution happening inside of you and me
</p>
