<p>
The Morning
So full of hope and promise,<br>
At first light<br>
The sprouting of that seed<br>
Divine,<br>
So gently....<br>
It meets the sound of birds;<br>
The sky<br>
The earth<br>
All rejoice<br>
In Her sweet loving presence<br>
</p>
