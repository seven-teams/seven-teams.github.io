<p style="text-align:center;">
The Dream Lives On<br>
<br>
As the lion roars one more time<br>
With kindness and for justice<br>
For rights and righteousness<br>
and not just laws<br>
<br>
The dream lives on<br>
<br>
As the banner,<br>
carried high for so long<br>
handed to a new generation<br>
flies proudly in the wind<br>
<br>
The dream lives on<br>
As we fight<br>
not with swords and guns<br>
not with words and dirty tricks<br>
but with the love and strength of doing good<br>
<br>
The dream lives on<br>
<br>
As our hearts are full of hope<br>
As our minds are full of confidence<br>
that the future is ours, and it’s a better one<br>
<br>
The dream lives on<br>
As the lion roars one more time
</p>
