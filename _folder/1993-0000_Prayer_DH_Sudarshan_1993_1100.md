<p>
May you find peace,<br>
Oh children of the Himalayas,<br>
Naughty angels,<br>
Rolicking and Frolicking<br>
In the lap of Holy Mother.<br>
<br>
Bold ones, Bursting with the joy of life,<br>
In you we see<br>
our need to learn obedience,<br>
To bow to love Divine,<br>
And bowing to rise,<br>
To see your blessed destiny unfold.<br>
<br>
From every corner of the globe<br>
Sri Hanuman has brought you.<br>
So lovingly,<br>
Shri Mataji Herself has taught you.<br>
No ill befall you.<br>
Sustained by Love<br>
that will not fail you,<br>
<br>
In your hands<br>
May righteousness prevail.<br>
May innocence<br>
Pul down its mighty roots,<br>
Produce abundant verdant shoots,<br>
Flowers,<br>
Fruits,<br>
A harvest of joyl<br>
<br>
Now in your lofty nest,
Grow strong;<br>
The world awaits the beauty of your song!
</p>
