<p>
O Mother earth<br>
Forgive us, forgive our race<br>
Forgive me too<br>
For touching Thee with my feet.<br>
Thou, the essence of all fragrance<br>
May Thou be gratified<br>
By the perfume of the new lotuses.<br>
</p>
