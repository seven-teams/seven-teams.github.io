<p>
In rainy season when we look around we come to know about the actual beauty of nature. Nature's beauty cannot be seen at every time. This can only be seen when rain drops clean the leaves of trees and plants. When these leaves are covered with drops they come down like a stream of river Ganges.<br>
<br>
As soon as these drops full on the ground, they are lost in the earth. When the rain stops, the black clouds leave the sky and go to heaven to rest. When these black clouds are gone[,] the sun, one by one, sets it's rays free. When the rays get freedom, they become so happy that they mix up with the rest of the hanging droplets in the sky and form a RAINBOW.<br>
<br>
When the sun rays reach the ground, the birds, which hide themselves in the shade of the trees to protect themselves from rain, come out. Birds' chirping and the fragrance of the soil after the rain, win the hearts of the people. When the rain droplets wash all the dust from the leaves, they become as fresh as if they've just newly sprouted. Rivers coming out of the mountains take a lot of soil with them, and so make so their water muddy. Plants and trees by the river sides are benifited [benefited] with their waters. It seems that the dark green colour of the leaves and brown trunks of the trees, all are enjoying rain drops. After rain, the flowers of the plants look fresh and happy. Red, yellow and white flowers are adding to the beauty of the plants. Some drops accumulate in the flowers and butterflies and honey bees take them to satisfy their thirst. Sometimes when nobody takes these drops they fell down like God's river.<br> 
<br>
Now when the sun is bright in the sky, the scene which the rain gave me is still in front of my eyes just as the cow and the calf can never be separated. Sun rays which were once imprisoned by the black clouds, are now again showing their strength. Now the dream of this beauty is slowly fading away. The fragrance of the earth is also going away with the wind. This beauty is only GOD'S GIFT.
</p>
