<p>
Be constant with the way<br>
God’s love is so great<br>
you come to him in your own freedom<br>
be constant with the way<br>
the way of vibrations<br>
the way of Paramchaitanya<br>
if you are that<br>
then that is what you are<br>
be constant with the way<br>
you do not force<br>
for it is not the way<br>
it is God’s love<br>
it is the way<br>
be constant with the way.<br>
</p>
