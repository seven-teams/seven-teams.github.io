<p>
They are only thoughts<br>
These clouds that bind<br>
They are not real<br>
Rise above mind.<br>
<br>
Open your heart<br>
Here are your wings<br>
Join the music<br>
All creation sings.<br>
<br>
Smell the sunlight<br>
Coaxing the rose<br>
Feel the wind bathing<br>
Your head, fingers, toes.<br>
<br>
Here is the Freedom<br>
And Joy to embrace<br>
It is sweetest honor<br>
To touch this grace
</p>
