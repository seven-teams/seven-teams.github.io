<p>
She walks<br>
<br>
I sit and gaze unblinking into the fire<br>
<br>
She burns<br>
<br>
I lay silent in my still bed listening<br>
<br>
She sings I turn my head to the mirror<br>
<br>
She reflects I absorb<br>
<br>
She does all this for me I bow<br>
<br>
She knows I doubt<br>
<br>
She forgives I cry in my bed like a small child<br>
<br>
She lifts me up I am down She is old I am young<br>
<br>
She walks though Her body aches<br>
<br>
She walks and walks She is here for us all<br>
<br>
She roars I open my mouth to speak words come slowly<br>
<br>
She passes leaving the smell of flowers<br>
<br>
I follow I pick up mu words like flowers stumbling in Her wake<br>
<br>
She walks.<br>
<br>
She loves I yearn She shows<br>
<br>
I learn little by little<br>
<br>
I walk behind Her Until the day<br>
<br>
I walk beside Her.
</p>
