<p style="text-align:center;">
Human beings<br>
have divided up the world<br>
into large numbers of religions<br>
<br>
Not only into those who profess<br>
to know the Gods Divine<br>
<br>
Also into those who profess to know<br>
the Gods of Science<br>
the Gods of Evolution<br>
<br>
They know that they are right<br>
It is the others who are wrong<br>
<br>
They could not possibly believe<br>
what the others do makes any sense<br>
<br>
They would betray their kind<br>
if they would allow themselves<br>
to consider any of the others methods<br>
<br>
The Great Spirit<br>
the all-pervading power<br>
does not know of such divisions<br>
<br>
Even the Spirit within<br>
a reflection of the Great in us<br>
does not know of these divisions either<br>
<br>
Placed inside each and every heart<br>
Love is the only method that it knows<br>
Desires for unity and recognition are<br>
the only desires that can awaken it<br>
<br>
Seeing the different reflections of the Great<br>
in oneself and others<br>
<br>
Eventually leads us to understanding<br>
Eventually allows us to love<br>
ourselves and others
</p>
