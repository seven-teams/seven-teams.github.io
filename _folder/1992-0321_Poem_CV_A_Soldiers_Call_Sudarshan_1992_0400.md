<br>

<p style="text-align:center;">
In the distance, we hear conches<br>
Blowing us to silent heights;<br>
Missiles wait in golden launches<br>
Your Command to win the fight.<br>
<br>
Now we glimpse the Shakti powers<br>
Shimmering inside our frames;<br>
Breezy fields of radiant flowers<br>
Safe from satan's evil games.<br>
<br>
From You we've gained God's weaponry;<br>
Fearless, doubtless, lovingly;<br>
Lost sons and daughters we'll help free<br>
To know vibrations' rhapsody.<br>
<br>
Humbly Your Knights beseech Thee;<br>
How to help them reach the skies?<br>
(Exposes depth a wee bit wobbly;<br>
When widsom's [wisdom's] firm no queries rise).<br>
<br>
Your Incarnation is our life;<br>
Surrender's joy; compassion's peace;<br>
To fuse with You gives end to strife;<br>
Wondrous miracles ne'er to cease.
</p>
