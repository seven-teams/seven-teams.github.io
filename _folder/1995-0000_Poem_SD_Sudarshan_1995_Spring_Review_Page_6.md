<p>
SHRI KRISHNA please come out and play<br>
There are lots of things to do<br>
<br>
Brighten up the world<br>
Brighten up the sky<br>
Brighten up everyone too<br>
<br>
If you don't come out<br>
we feel sad and blue<br>
without you---<br>
<br>
So please Shri Krishna<br>
please Shri Krishna<br>
Come out and clear us too!<br>
</p>
