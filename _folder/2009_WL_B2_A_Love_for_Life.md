<p style="text-align:center;">
A love for life<br>
Nurture the joy in the heart<br>
Keep your minds on each other<br>
<br>
Caring leads to understanding<br>
Understanding leads to<br>
knowledge of your needs<br>
Love will grow stronger every day<br>
<br>
A love for life<br>
Keep the creator in your heart<br>
Nurture the joy of the light within<br>
<br>
By Seeking the eternal<br>
Love leads to understanding<br>
Understanding leads to knowledge<br>
And faith will grow stronger every day<br>
<br>
A love for life<br>
May the heart guide you on your way<br>
May the eternal be your goal
</p>
