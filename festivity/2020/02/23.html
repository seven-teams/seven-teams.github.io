      2020 Mahasamadhi Day

      2 minute read

      Tag: Paramachaitanya Day


      Thank You for letting us participate in the salvation of mankind.
      Thank You for giving us the joy of introducing a yearning seeker to Your redeeming Motherly Love.
      Thank You for inviting all of us to the court of the Goddess.
      Thank You for establishing the Kingdom of God on Earth.
      Thank You for accepting our offerings and prayers.
      Thank You for showering Your blessings endlessly upon us.

      "My vision for Sahaja Yoga is that, that all the good people, righteous people,
      God-loving people will rise above all that is useless, worldly,
      and reside in the Heavens with all the blessings of God, forever and ever."
      1987-0506 Talk to Sahaja Yogis, Burwood Ashram, Sydney

      Thank You for opening the gateway to Heaven within us.
      Thank You for bestowing the gift of yoga upon us.
      Thank You for making us.
      Thank You for baptizing us.
      Thank You for healing us.
      Thank You for purifying us.
      Thank You for being ever-present.
      Thank You for being gentle.
      Thank You for being wonderful.
      Thank You for being in our hearts.
      Thank You for being our Guru.
      Thank You for being our Mother.

      "I am with you at every step, at every place. Everywhere. Wherever you are, at whichever place you are, I am with you, completely, in person, by My Spirit and by My word completely. This is My promise to you. But those who wish to go to Hell, they will also be sucked down. That is also arranged. Therefore be careful. Take to higher life.
      ......
      Even for a moment, I shall not be late but you will have to be Mine. This is very important. If you are Mine then I shall not be late even for a moment. I shall be before you.
      May God bless you all and give you wisdom."
      1976-0527 Public Program (Hindi), Bharatiya Vidyā Bhavan, Gita Mandir Hall, Chowpatty, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India
