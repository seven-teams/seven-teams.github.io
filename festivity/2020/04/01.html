Now these two sounds create two kinds of vibrations. I would say the mantra for the right-hand side is ‘Ham’ and for the left-hand side is ‘Kṣham’.

If you have such a lot of ego you must all the time try to humble down in your heart. Now in the heart resides the Spirit. The ego, too much of ego in a person, always takes you away from that Spirit. That’s why it is said, “Humble down in your heart."
      1978-1218 Public Program, Caxton Hall, Westminster, London, UK
