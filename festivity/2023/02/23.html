      2023 Celebrating Mahāsamādhi Day: “I am the Holy Ghost, I am the Holy Spirit, who has incarnated on this earth for your Realization.”

      7 minute read

      Tags: Paramachaitanya Day


      "Many Sahaj Yogis are sitting here. I am telling something new for them. If you are Sahaja Yogis, then it should be expressed in your behavior. Every one should know that you are Sahaja Yogis. We should know that a person is either a Sahaja Yogi or a non-Sahaja Yogi. We should know the art of integrating all. With this Sahaj Swabhāv you should conquer the whole world.
      Register in your heart what I have said.
      This is My vision. You people could fulfill and relieve Me of My heartache."
      2000-0407 Śhrī Bhūmī Devī Pūjā (Hindi), Inauguration of the H.H. Śhrī Mātājī Nirmalā Devī Foundation, Nirmal Dham, Behind BSF Camp Chhawala, New Delhi, India

      Thank You for revealing to us within the human body the reflection of the Vishwa-rupa (universal form).
      Thank You for revealing to us the primordial principles of creation.
      Thank You for revealing to us the reality of human nature.
      Thank You for revealing to us the meaning of true religion.
      Thank You for teaching us the divine knowledge of Sahaja Yoga.

      MAVENA NAYANI ANAND MAVENA NAYANI ANAND, HRUDAYAT SATCHITANAND
      I am unable to contain the Joy in my eyes when in my heart resides the joy of the Spirit
      ANAND KITI HA MOTHA, SAUKHYAS KHADHI NA TOTA
      How great this joy is, which brings limitless bliss
      KANA— KANAT NIRMALANAND HRUDAYAT SATCHITANAND
      For every particle is manifesting sheer joy, when in my heart resides the joy of the Spirit
      SAGUNAT MAULI ĀLI, NIRGUNAT JANĪV DIDHALI
      Mother incarnated to raise our awareness to feel the formless,
      NIJARUPI RANGALE DHYAN, JADATET MATE CHAITANYA
      As we meditate upon Her, we discovered the chaitanya in this mundane world
      video

      "My vision for Sahaja Yoga is that, that all the good people, righteous people,
      God-loving people will rise above all that is useless, worldly, and reside in the Heavens with all the blessings of God, forever and ever."
      1987-0506 Talk to Sahaja Yogis, The Need to Go Deeper and Questions and Answers, Āśhram, 10 Clarence Street, Burwood, Sydney, New South Wales, Australia

      Thank You for giving Sahaja Yoga to a confused and self-destructive humanity.
      Thank You for revealing to us the meaning of creation and evolution.
      Thank You for revealing to us the actual existence of God.
      Thank You for giving divine meaning to human existence.

      Ap.Ne Dil Me

      Let the Creator's creation be created again in you

      Kuṇḍalinī Tum Ap.Ni Jagaya Karo

      Thand. Chaitanya Swayam Bahaya Karo

      Thank You for giving us meditation.
      Thank You for giving us inner peace.
      Thank You for giving us inner joy.
      Thank You for giving us enlightened attention.
      Thank You for giving us, detachment.

      PRABHU MAZA MALA JEVHA BASE GHEUNI EKANTĪ
      When I am alone with my God (in meditation)
      MITI VARNU MALA TEVHA GHADE JI HO SUKHAPRAPTĪ
      The bliss that I feel is beyond words
      MALA PĀHĪ PRABHUSANGE, DUI ATMA TAI PALE DURĪ
      When I am in the company of God, evil runs away
      GALE SAMARTHYA MOHACHE NURE RUNT KUTHE VAIRĪ
      No temptation can dominate me and nobody can confront me
      MALA BODHĀMRUT PĀJĪ, SADA ]AEI NAVI TRUPTĪ
      Every time I experience the Divine knowledge, I feel more satisfaction and Divine Joy
      KUTHE CHINTA KUTHE DUKHHE KSHANE SĀRI LAVA JĀTĪ
      All moments of concerns and sorrows vanish
      PRABHUSANGE MILE JĪVA, NASE KOTHE TASHI SHANTĪ
      Nowhere else can my soul find peace except in the company (in Union) with God Almighty
      PRABHU MAZA SWAYE NĪTĪ, PRABHU MAZA SWAYE PRITĪ
      My God himself is Truth and Love

      "I am with you at every step, at every place. Everywhere. Wherever you are, at whichever place you are, I am with you, completely, in person, by My Spirit and by My word completely. This is My promise to you.
      ......
      But you have to be Mine. It's important you have to be Mine, then in just seconds I will be with you. Let Paramātma give you joy (sukhī) and give you pure knowledge (subuddhi). Immense blessings!"
      1976-0529 Talk to Sahaja Yogis (Hindi), Dhyān Kaise Karein (How to Meditate), Gita Mandir Hall, Bharatiya Vidyā Bhavan (3rd floor), 29, Kulapati K.M. Munshi Mārg, Chowpatty, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

      Thank You for Your continuous attention.
      Thank You for being the mirror.
      Thank You for being ever present.
      Thank You for being gentle.
      Thank You for being wonderful.

      Thank You for guiding us.
      Thank You for comforting us.
      Thank You for counselling us.
      Thank You for correcting us.
      Thank You for never abandoning us.

      HE PRABHO TU DNYANA RUPE, KALPAVRUKSHA LAVILE
      O God, You in the form of knowledge have planted the tree that fulfils all desires (kalpataru)
      BAHARALYA SHAKHA TAYANCHYA, CHINTANI MAN. RANGALE
      The branches of the tree have blossomed; my mind has completely dissolved in it
      VISHWA HE SAMAVILE
      It has completely embraced the whole universe

      SANSKRUTI GANGAUGH VAHE, TYAMADHE AWAGAWUNI
      The river of pure Sahaj culture is flowing and in that we are immersed
      SHODHANYA SHIV. SATYA. SUNDAR. , DIVYA SHRADDHA GHEVUNI
      We are in search of the truth, beauty and the spirit with an enlightened faith
      CHALALE PATH. SADHANECHA, GURU PADATE PAVALE
      We are on the path of seeking, following the Holy feet of the Guru

      Tere Charan.kamal. me Rehnevalo me Likh.le Mera Nam
      May my name be added to the one of those who are seeking refuge at Thy Lotus Feet,
      Ye Hai Sukh. Shanti Ka Dham
      For this is the heavenly abode of Joy eternal and Peace,
      Nirmal Dham, Nirmal Dham, re
      The abode of Divinity and Purity.
      Aya Tha Shraddha Suman. Charhane Dale Jholi Me
      I have come to offer the flowers of pure faith at the Lotus Feet of Our Mother,
      Ma Se Paunga Mai Kuchh. Dan. Bhi Dalu Jhole Me
      Expecting to be rewarded for my offering,
      Saumya Prakash. Chet.na Dekhi
      But I found myself overwhelmed by the pleasant Chaitanya,
      Jaise Bhor.ki Lali Me
      Radiating like a rising sun.
      Swar.pan. Kar.ke Bola Mai
      Surrendering myself to Her, I humbly requested
      Kuchh. Na Dalu Jholi Me
      That no reward should be given,
      Nir.peksh. Yahi Bhakti Kutir Hai Chaitanya Ka Aisa Dham
      As I realized that worship can only exist through selfless surrender to the Lotus Feet of Śhrī Mātājī, the very abode of Chaitanya.
