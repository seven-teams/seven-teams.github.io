      2021 Celebrating Śhrī Ādi Śhakti Pūjā: Evening program before Śhrī Ādi Śhakti Pūjā

      3 minute read

      Tag: Shri Adi Shakti Puja


      "On the subject of the Ādi Śhakti, every religion will come in. So for the variety entertainment, there should be the subject of the Ādi Śhakti."
      1975-0120 Talk to Sahaja Yogis (Hindi), Day 1, Session 2 (evening), Kṣham Ki Śhakti Ka Mahatav (Power of Forgiveness) or Dharm va Adharm (Dharma and Adharma), Gita Mandir Hall, Bharatiya Vidyā Bhavan (3rd floor), 29, Kulapati K.M. Munshi Mārg, Chowpatty, Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

      SAHASRĀRĪ VANDUYA NIRMALĀ MĀTĀ DEYIJĪ SARVA SAHAJA YOGA
      Let us worship Śhrī Mātājī at our Sahasrāra, Who is the giver of Sahaja Yoga
      ZAG.VI TYA KUNDALINI SARV. JANANCHYA SOD.VI TYA JANMANCHYA FERYA
      She is awakening the Kuṇḍalinī of everyone, snaking us free from the circle of birth and death

      BHUVARI ALI, ĀDI ŚHAKTI MĀTĀ, UDDHARANYA SARV. JANA
      The Primordial Mother has taken birth on this Earth, for the salvation of the whole world
      RUP. TE SAJI RE, NIRMALĀ MĀTĀ ZAG.VI SACCHI DANANDA
      How beautiful You are, Mother Nirmalā who has awakened Satchitananda
      NAMAN. NIRMALĀ MĀTĀ, TUMHA NAMAN. NIRMALĀ MĀTĀ
      Salutations to You, Mother Nirmalā!
      SAHASRAKOTI SURYACHE TEJ . SHOBHATASE TUMCHYA VAD. NA
      The glory of hundred thousand suns is shining on Your face
      DHARI YELYA SARV. DEV.TA TUMHI NAMI TI TUM. CHYA CHAR. NA
      (You are bearing) all the Gods and Goddesses Bow down at Your Lotus Feet</font>
      Naman Nirmal Mātā
      video

      All sciences are part of You. The feminine power of all women is from You.
      By You alone, O Great Mother, by You alone this world is filled.
      How can we sing Your praise when You are beyond praise? All the sublime words are deficient.

      The Goddess said: And whoever, with full attention, regularly praises me with these hymns, I will quiet every trouble for him.
      From the Devī Mahātmyam
      Shrī Nirmalā Chalisa
      video

      Vishw'eshwari twam paripasi vishwam
      O Goddess of the universe, You are its protector.
      Vishw'atmika dharayas iti vishwam
      You are the universe, You are its support
      Vishw'esha-vandya Bhavati bhavanti
      You are worshipped by the Lord of the universe.
      Vishw'ashraya ye twayi bhakti-namraha
      Those who are devoted to You themselves become a refuge for all.
      Prana-tanam prasida twam
      O Devī, the remover of the afflictions of universe,
      Devī Vishw'arti-harini
      Be pleased with us who prostrate themselves at Your Feet.
      Trailokya-vasinam'idye
      O Devī, who are worthy of praise by all the dwellers of three worlds,
      Lokanam varada bhava
      Please grant boons to all the people
      From the Devī Mahātmyam
      (Chapter 11, Narayani stotram, shlokas 33 and 35)

      He Ādi Mā, He Anti Mā
      video

      Śhrī Ādi Śhakti, to describe You is the work of poets and Saints, yet words cannot even begin to express Your magnificence.

      You are Paraśhakti, the Power beyond all powers.

      Give us, O Śhrī Ādi Śhakti, greater humility so that we can gain some small glimpse of Your glory.

      Make us like the Sufis and the Gnostics adoring You at every moment.

      Thank You, O Śhrī Ādi Śhakti, for the seeking which brought us to Sahaja Yoga.
      You now lift up humanity into the omega of the Last Judgement.
      Adorations to Śhrī Ādi Śhakti (Mantra Book, Page 231)
