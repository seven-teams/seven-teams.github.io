      2021 Offering our humble gratitude at the Lotus Feet of our Beloved Mother

      7 minute read

      Tag: Paramachaitanya Day


      "My vision for Sahaja Yoga is that, that all the good people, righteous people, God-loving people will rise above all that is useless, worldly, and reside in the Heavens with all the blessings of God, forever and ever."
      1987-0506 Talk to Sahaja Yogis, Burwood Ashram, Sydney

      RACHA PRABHU TUNEY YAHA BRAHMANDA SARA
      Oh God You have created the whole universe
      PRANON SEY PYARA, BRAHMANDA SARA
      The whole universe is dearer than our lives
      TUHI MAI BANDHU, TUHI JAGAT JANANI
      You are my Mother and brother, You are the Mother of the universe
      SAKAL JAGAT MEIN YAH TERA PASARA
      The whole universe is but your form
      video
      There is a lot of surrendering in this song and Śhrī Mātājī told the yogis to learn the song as it makes you surrendered. This song has to be sung with the awareness of the greatness of God and thus a surrendered heart surrendered to the greatness of God.
      When singing we have to be conscious (janeev.) that the entire universe has been created by Him. "Who are we ?"
      (from 'Matru Snehita')

      MAVENA NAYANI ANAND
      MAVENA NAYANI ANAND, HRUDAYAT SATCHITANAND

      How great this joy is, which brings limitless bliss

      Tere Charan.kamal. me Rehnevalo me Likh.le Mera Nam
      May my name be added to the one of those who are seeking refuge at Thy Lotus Feet,
      Ye Hai Sukh. Shanti Ka Dham
      For this is the heavenly abode of Joy eternal and Peace,
      Nirmal Dham, Nirmal Dham, re
      The abode of Divinity and Purity.
      Aya Tha Shraddha Suman. Charhane Dale Jholi Me
      I have come to offer the flowers of pure faith at the Lotus Feet of Our Mother,
      Ma Se Paunga Mai Kuchh. Dan. Bhi Dalu Jhole Me
      Expecting to be rewarded for my offering,
      Saumya Prakash. Chet.na Dekhi
      But I found myself overwhelmed by the pleasant Chaitanya,
      Jaise Bhor.ki Lali Me
      Radiating like a rising sun.
      Swar.pan. Kar.ke Bola Mai
      Surrendering myself to Her, I humbly requested
      Kuchh. Na Dalu Jholi Me
      That no reward should be given,
      Nir.peksh. Yahi Bhakti Kutir Hai Chaitanya Ka Aisa Dham
      As I realized that worship can only exist through selfless surrender to the Lotus Feet of Śhrī Mātājī, the very abode of Chaitanya.
      video

      Dear All,
      Today, on the Mahasamadhi Day of the complete Incarnation of Śhrī Adi Shakti Mātājī Śhrī Nirmalā Devī, with folded hands and bended knees and in complete love and awe and gratitude, we remember the Earthly life of our Divine Mother and all She has done for the evolution, redemption, and spiritual uplifting of mankind.

      It is a time to re-commit and re-dedicate ourselves to Her Visions expressed in Her teachings, to the work of Sahaja Yoga, and to the advancement of Vishwa Nirmala Dharma and of Sahaja Culture. We pray to Śhrī Mātājī to help us to accomplish all Her Divine Visions.
      With all these in mind, we propose to watch the documentary 'Vision'
